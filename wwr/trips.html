Thunder Gorge

An adrenaline-pumping run through towering canyon walls and Class V rapids. Expect turbulent waters, steep drops, and unforgettable views that test your paddle power and reward your courage.

Rattlesnake Drop

A swift descent through roaring currents and tight switchbacks. This intermediate run offers a perfect balance of technical maneuvering and scenic riverbanks lined with old-growth pines.

The Widowmaker's Bend

Not for the faint of heart — this legendary bend lives up to its name. Heart-racing currents challenge even the most seasoned paddlers while revealing dramatic rock formations and hidden coves.

Devil's Grin

Brace yourself for a wild ride—Devil's Grin teases with calm waters before unleashing chaos. Sudden drops, swirling hydraulics, and deceptive eddies keep paddlers on edge, while narrow chutes and sheer rock faces demand razor-sharp focus. Survive it, and you'll understand why it’s smiling.

Echo Falls

Graceful but untamed, Echo Falls roars with beauty and power. Cascading tiers of whitewater challenge your reflexes while echoing off canyon walls in a symphony of thunder. Between bursts of adrenaline, catch glimpses of mist-draped cliffs and tranquil pools hidden just beyond the spray.
