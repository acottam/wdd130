Thunder Gorge

Roar through steep canyon corridors where Class V rapids test your reflexes and resolve. Thunder Gorge delivers heart-pounding action, explosive whitewater, and panoramic views that reward every daring paddle stroke.

Rattlesnake Drop

Slither through churning currents and sharp switchbacks on this intermediate thrill ride. Rattlesnake Drop combines agile turns with bursts of whitewater, framed by towering evergreens and wild riverbanks.

The Widowmaker's Bend

A legendary name with the rapids to match. The Widowmaker’s Bend dares even seasoned paddlers to thread narrow channels and punch through raging waves—rewarding the bold with hidden coves and haunting canyon echoes.

Devil's Grin

It starts with a smile—and ends in chaos. Devil’s Grin lulls paddlers with calm waters before unleashing drops, whirlpools, and surprise reversals. It's a test of nerve and navigation, capped off with a grin only the bold will understand.

Echo Falls

As beautiful as it is brutal, Echo Falls cascades in rhythmic tiers of churning whitewater. Rapids echo off the canyon walls in a thunderous chorus while mist veils hidden cliffs and calm eddies just beyond the chaos.
