History

SPLASH White Water Rafting was born from a wild idea on the banks of the roaring Rogue River, where a group of thrill-seekers traded their office chairs for paddles and never looked back. What started as a weekend passion turned into a full-blown mission: to share the pulse-pounding joy of whitewater with adventurers from all walks of life. From our first homemade raft to now leading thousands down Class III-V rapids, we've made it our calling to deliver unforgettable rides, epic splashes, and stories that last a lifetime.
